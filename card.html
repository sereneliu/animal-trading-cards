Sea Turtle

Did you know that a sea turtle's gender is determined by how hot or cold their environment was back when they were still in their eggs? Warmer = female; cooler = male!

  • Scientific Name: Chelonioidea
  • Species of Sea Turtles: 7
  • Average Lifespan: ~80
  • Habitat: the ocean

Sea turtles spend most of their lives in the water, some traveling over 10,000 miles a year.
